Who We Are

Josiah Girl's Secondary School stands as a beacon of excellence in girls' education in Bukoba, Kagera, Tanzania. We offer comprehensive Ordinary Level (Form 1 to Form 4) and Advanced Level (Form 5 to Form 6) programs designed to transform young women into tomorrow's leaders.

Our mission goes beyond academics - we create an environment where every girl discovers her unique potential, develops unshakable confidence, and prepares for a future filled with possibilities. With a perfect blend of rigorous curriculum and character development, we ensure our students graduate not just with outstanding results, but with the skills and vision to make a meaningful impact.

At Josiah, we understand that educating a girl changes everything. That's why we're committed to providing an experience that nurtures the mind, body, and spirit, preparing our students to excel in university, careers, and life.

Exceptional Girls Education

Our distinguished faculty members are more than teachers - they're mentors who bring passion, expertise, and innovative teaching methods to every classroom. With small class sizes and personalized attention, we ensure each student achieves her academic potential while developing critical thinking, creativity, and leadership skills essential for success in today's world.

Ideal Learning Environment

Our beautiful campus is designed to inspire excellence. Bright, spacious classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, quiet study areas, and lush green spaces create the perfect atmosphere for academic focus and personal growth. Modern dormitories provide comfortable living spaces, while our dining hall serves nutritious meals to keep our students energized and healthy.

Cutting-Edge Technology

We invest in the future with state-of-the-art computer labs, science laboratories equipped with the latest technology, and smart classrooms that enhance the learning experience. Our students develop advanced digital literacy skills that prepare them for university and careers in our rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Our Commitment

As Tanzania's premier all-girls institution, Josiah Girl's Secondary School specializes in understanding and meeting the unique educational needs of young women. Our approach combines academic rigor with personal development, creating well-rounded individuals prepared for life's challenges.

Located in the scenic Bukoba region of Kagera, our school offers more than just education - we provide a transformative experience. Our comprehensive programs include leadership training, career guidance, health education, and community service opportunities that build character and confidence.

We take pride in our holistic approach that nurtures intellectual curiosity, physical well-being, and emotional resilience. From championship-winning sports teams to award-winning debate clubs, Josiah girls excel in all areas of school life. Our alumnae network includes trailblazers in medicine, engineering, business, and public service across Tanzania and beyond.
