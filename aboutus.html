About Our School

Josiah Girl's Secondary School is a premier single-sex institution for girls located in the beautiful region of Bukoba, Kagera, Tanzania, just 6 kilometers from Bukoba Municipal Town. We are dedicated to providing a safe, supportive environment where young women can thrive academically, socially, and personally.

Our comprehensive approach to education ensures that every student not only achieves excellent examination results but also develops strong character, positive social behaviors, and essential life skills that will serve them well beyond their school years.

Hon. Madam Headmistress

"At Josiah Girls, we don't just teach subjects - we shape futures. Our mission is to cultivate confident, capable young women who will become leaders in their communities and role models for generations to come. Education is the foundation upon which we build empowered women who will transform our society."

Dedicated Faculty

Our school takes pride in its team of highly qualified, passionate teachers who are committed to each student's success. With small class sizes and personalized attention, our educators go beyond teaching to mentor and inspire every girl to reach her highest potential.

Comprehensive Curriculum

We follow the NECTA syllabuses while enriching our program with additional courses that develop critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills. This balanced approach ensures our students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for both academic and life success.

Holistic Development

Our educational philosophy recognizes that true success encompasses more than academic achievement. We nurture the complete individual, fostering social skills, emotional intelligence, and ethical values that create well-rounded young women ready to make meaningful contributions to society.

Our Achievements & Awards

Josiah Girl's Secondary School has consistently ranked among Tanzania's top-performing girls' schools. Our commitment to excellence has been recognized through numerous awards and accolades celebrating our students' outstanding academic achievements and all-around development.

Our Educational Programmes

Josiah Girl's Secondary School offers a structured, progressive educational program designed specifically for young women. Our curriculum combines academic rigor with practical skill development, ensuring our students are well-prepared for higher education and professional careers.

Ordinary Level (O-Level)

Our four-year O-Level program builds a strong academic foundation while developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills essential for success in advanced studies and beyond.

Form One & Form Two

The first two years focus on establishing core competencies across all subjects. Students explore various disciplines to identify their strengths and interests while developing essential study habits and learning strategies. Our supportive environment helps girls transition smoothly into secondary education.

Form Three & Form Four

In the final O-Level years, students deepen their subject knowledge while preparing for national examinations. We provide intensive coaching, regular assessments, and personalized support to ensure each girl performs to her fullest potential. Our comprehensive preparation gives students confidence to excel in their exams.

Advanced Level (A-Level)

Our two-year A-Level program offers specialized combinations that prepare students for university education and professional careers. We provide advanced resources, research opportunities, and mentorship to help students excel in their chosen fields.

Form Five & Form Six

These crucial years represent the pinnacle of secondary education. Students engage in in-depth study of their chosen subjects, developing advanced analytical skills and subject mastery. Our expert faculty provides guidance for university preparation and career planning, ensuring each student is ready for her next academic challenge.

Core Subjects (O-Level)

All students from Form One to Form Four study these essential subjects:

Mathematics English Kiswahili Biology Civics Physics Chemistry Geography History

A-Level Combinations

Students can choose from these specialized subject combinations:

PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)

Ideal for students pursuing engineering, physics, or mathematics at university level.

PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)

Perfect for future doctors, pharmacists, and other medical professionals.

PGM ,CBG (Physics, Geography, Mathematics) (chemistry, biology ,mathematics)

Excellent preparation for careers in environmental science, geology, or architecture.

HGL ,HGK,HGE,HKL,(History,English, Geography, Language)

For students interested in law, international relations, or social sciences.

ECA EGM,(Economics, Commerce, Accounting,Geography,Mathematics)

Prepares students for business, finance, and economics degrees.
