Admission Process

Form Five Admission

We are delighted to announce admissions for Form Five students for the 2025/2026 academic year. At Josiah Girls, we provide an exceptional learning environment where young women thrive in their advanced level studies.

Prospective students must have completed Form Four national examinations (CSEE) with a minimum of C credit in all subjects relevant to their desired combination. We are currently accepting applications, and parents are encouraged to enroll their daughters for this transformative phase of their education.

Our comprehensive admission process includes both written assessments and oral interviews. This allows us to understand each student's unique strengths and learning needs, ensuring we can provide the personalized support necessary for academic excellence in advanced level studies.

At Josiah Girls, we don't just educate - we inspire, challenge, and prepare young women for leadership. Our advanced level program combines rigorous academics with character development, creating well-rounded graduates ready to make their mark on the world.

Form Six Enrollment

For students progressing to Form Six, enrollment begins six months prior to national examinations. This strategic timing ensures continuity in learning and allows students to build on the strong foundation established in Form Five.

Students continue with their chosen combinations from Form Five, benefiting from our specialized teaching methodologies tailored for A-Level success. Our program includes intensive revision sessions, mock examinations, and personalized feedback to maximize performance.

Each Form Six candidate undergoes a comprehensive evaluation including written tests and oral interviews. This thorough assessment helps us identify specific areas for improvement, allowing us to provide targeted support that ensures every student is fully prepared to excel in their national examinations.

Our Form Six program goes beyond exam preparation - we develop critical thinkers, problem solvers, and future leaders. With small class sizes and dedicated faculty, we provide the individual attention that makes the difference between good and exceptional results.

Form One & Form Three Enrollment

Josiah Girls Secondary School warmly welcomes new students to our Form One and Form Three classes. For Form One students beginning their secondary education in 2026, we offer a nurturing environment that makes the transition to high school both exciting and rewarding.

Our Form Three program provides the perfect opportunity for students to join our community as they prepare for their critical examination years. We offer comprehensive academic support and extracurricular activities to ensure well-rounded development.

We are committed to delivering excellence in education, combining academic rigor with character development. Our holistic approach ensures students grow intellectually, socially, and emotionally during these formative years of their education.

All prospective students participate in our assessment process designed to understand each girl's unique potential. This allows us to provide the personalized support that helps every student thrive academically and personally throughout their educational journey with us.

Form Two & Form Four Admission

For students seeking to join our Form Two and Form Four classes, we recommend enrollment before March to ensure adequate time to integrate with classmates and fully prepare for examinations.

Our Form Two program builds on foundational knowledge while introducing more complex concepts, preparing students for upper secondary challenges. Form Four students benefit from our intensive examination preparation program, including revision sessions, study skills workshops, and mock examinations.

The admission process includes assessments designed to evaluate each student's academic level and identify areas where additional support may be needed. This ensures we can provide the right resources to help every girl achieve her full potential.

At Josiah Girls, we believe every student deserves the opportunity to excel. Our supportive environment, experienced teachers, and comprehensive resources create the perfect conditions for academic achievement and personal growth at every level.
