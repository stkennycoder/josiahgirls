School Announcements

Form Six Graduation Ceremony 2025

May 25, 2025

We, the Josiah Girls Secondary School staff, warmly welcome all parents, guardians, and well-wishers to attend the graduation ceremony of our Form Six students. This momentous occasion celebrates their academic achievements and marks the beginning of their future endeavors.

Form Four & Form Two Mock Examinations

June 15, 2025

The school administration sincerely announces that the mock examinations for Form Four and Form Two students will commence on 15th June 2025. These tests are crucial for assessing preparedness and identifying areas for improvement before the national examinations.

Parents-Teachers Meeting

July 10, 2025

The school administration invites all parents to our quarterly meeting scheduled for 10th July 2025. This is an important opportunity to discuss your daughter's academic progress, behavior, and overall development with her teachers and school administrators.
